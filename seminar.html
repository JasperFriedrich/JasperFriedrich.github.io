Oxford Critical Theory Seminar

The Oxford Critical Theory seminar has been running since 2018 and aims to showcase a wide range of critical and radical political theory. The seminar is open to anyone inside and outside the university - if you would like to get updates about events, you can sign up to our mailing list by sending a blank email to critical.theory.seminar-subscribe@maillist.ox.ac.uk For Hilary Term 2023, we have the following events planned:

Week 2, Jan 24: Werner Bonefeld (University of York). Title: “On Real Abstraction: Wealth and Suffering”

Week 3, Jan 31: Neal Harris (Oxford Brookes). Title: "Critical Theory and Social Pathology"
